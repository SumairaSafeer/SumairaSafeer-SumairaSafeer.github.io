About

I'm a Computer Engineering graduate from COMSATS University Islamabad (CGPA 3.85/4.00), working at the intersection of deep learning and hardware — where a model's accuracy on paper means nothing until it runs reliably on constrained silicon in the field.

My final year project modified a YOLOv8 architecture with CBAM and SE attention plus BiFPN feature fusion, lifting defect-detection accuracy by 75 percent on a real pipeline-inspection dataset, then deployed it on a Raspberry Pi at roughly 10 milliseconds per image — proof that research-grade improvements can survive contact with edge hardware.

Outside of deep learning, I build across the full embedded stack: Verilog on FPGA, FreeRTOS multitasking, digital signal processing, and network infrastructure design. I'm currently deepening that range through two concurrent AI internships, and looking toward a fully funded MS in Embedded AI and research collaborations that push efficient intelligence further into the physical world.
